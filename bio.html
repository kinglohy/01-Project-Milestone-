BIOGRAPHY


The Richie Lohy Experience was started by Richie Lohy. Richie was born in 1983 in Haarlem. Got a guitar pushed into his hands early in life. Received his first lesson from his mother. In the years that followed, he started taking lessons seriously and studying a lot in his room at home. Over the years Richie started playing in various pop, rock, blues bands. And now in 2021 he has started his own band called The Richie Lohy Experience. He does this together with Bassist Wilco Van Esschoten and Drummer Nico Eijlers. Together they form a Blues Power Trio inspired by Jimi Hendrix, SRV and John Mayer. Their first single will be released soon. Stay tuned!
